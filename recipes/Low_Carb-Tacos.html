A Recipe of a Low Carb Tacos meal

This low carb Mexican tacos meal is packed full of nutrients to fill you up without affecting your weight. It is colorful and the different textures of the different ingredients makes is suitable for the most sophisticated palates.


Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • ½ (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • ½ cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar Cheese
  • ¼ cup salsa
  • ¼ cup low-fat sour cream
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced

Directions of preparation

  1. Cook and stir ground beef, onion and jalapeno peppers together in a skillet over medium-high heat until meat is browned and crumbly. for 7 to 10 minute.
  2. Stir taco seasonng into meat mixture, bring to a simmer and cook until flavors combine for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir meat mixture, shredded lettuce, tomato, Cheddar cheese, salsa and sour cream together in a large bowl.
  4. Divide taco mixture amoung 4 bowls and top each with avocardo slices.
